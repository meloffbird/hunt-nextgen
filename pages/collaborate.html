GROUP STUDY ROOMS

Throughout the perimeter of the commons there are 18 group study rooms that can accomodate 4-5 people. They are equipped with large moniters that students can connect to via HDMI, which is useful when working on group presentations.

TABLES & PODS

Reservations for the group study rooms normally fill up within 24 hours but there are also 7-8 small to medium tables located in the middle of the learning commons. In addition, many students claim the "pods" as there favorite study spot. These partially-enclosed spaces fit 3-4 people and also include a connectable monitor but do not offer as much privacy as the group study rooms.
